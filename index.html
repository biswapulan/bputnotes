Questions

Frequently asked

Are the notes really free?▼

Yes. All notes and PYQs are completely free. Education should be accessible to every BPUT student without any barrier.

Who runs BPUTNotes?▼

BPUTNotes is entirely student-run. It was started by BPUT students, and is still managed and grown by a volunteer community across campuses.

Which branches have notes available?▼

Currently CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical and Civil. We're adding more branches every week. If yours isn't listed, reach out to us.

How is BPUTNotes different from BPUT's official portal?▼

We're an independent, student-built companion, not affiliated with BPUT University. Think of us as the study layer on top of the official system.
