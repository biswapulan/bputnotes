📖 How This Works

Choosing the right reference book

This isn't a replacement for your class notes — it's a companion. Reference textbooks go deeper into theory, worked examples and derivations that a semester's worth of lectures often can't cover in full. We list the books most BPUT departments actually recommend or follow for each subject, so you're not guessing which edition to buy or download.

If you're short on time before an exam, our subject notes and PYQs are the faster route — come back to these books when you want a proper conceptual foundation, not just exam prep.

Are these the exact books BPUT prescribes?

These are the reference books most commonly followed by BPUT faculty and toppers for each subject. Exact prescribed textbooks can vary slightly by college, so it's worth cross-checking with your department's syllabus if you need an exact match.

Do I need to buy these, or is there a free version linked?

Where a legitimate free or open copy exists, we link to it directly. For others, you'll need to source or purchase the book yourself — we only link to material we can share responsibly.

A subject on my syllabus isn't listed — what do I do?

Tap "Request a Book on WhatsApp" above with your branch, semester and subject name, and we'll try to source a recommendation.
