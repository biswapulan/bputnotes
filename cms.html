Syllabus Entry

Submit a new subject for the department syllabus. Duplicate entries are automatically blocked.

How to get a shareable link from Google Drive
  1. Upload the syllabus PDF to your own Google Drive.
  2. Right-click the file, choose Share, then set access to "Anyone with the link".
  3. Copy the link and paste it in the field below.
Make sure sharing is set to "Anyone with the link" before submitting.

Notes Entry

Upload study notes for a subject. Only one notes file is accepted per subject per semester.

How to get a shareable link from Google Drive
  1. Upload the notes PDF to your own Google Drive.
  2. Right-click the file, choose Share, then set access to "Anyone with the link".
  3. Copy the link and paste it in the field below.
Make sure sharing is set to "Anyone with the link" before submitting.
