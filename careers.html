✍️ Role Details

Academic Content Coordinator
— BPUTNotes.in

Your job is simple but impactful: collect question papers (PYQs) and subject notes from students, faculty, and online sources — then organize and submit them to your Content Manager. Your contributions go live on BPUTNotes and help thousands of BPUT students every day.

📋 How Your Work Flows — Day to Day
1
Collect PYQs & Notes Browse, gather, and scan previous year question papers and subject notes for assigned BPUT subjects — from classmates, seniors, library, or online sources.
2
Organize & Label Clearly Name files properly (Subject – Semester – Year) and ensure they are readable and complete before submitting. No half-scanned or blurry files.
3
Submit to Your Content Manager Send your collected files to your assigned Content Manager via WhatsApp or Google Drive. Your manager reviews and uploads them to the platform.
4
Stuck? Just Contact Your Manager Can't find a resource? Unsure about formatting? Your Content Manager is always available to guide you. You're never working alone.
